Why Web Development

Through expanding my web development skillset, I want to deepen my understanding of the fundamental building blocks of the internet—HTML, CSS, and JavaScript—and how these technologies come together to create the seamless digital experiences we rely on every day. Beyond mastering the basics, I am particularly excited about exploring how nascent technologies, such as artificial intelligence and the Internet of Things (IoT), can be integrated with web development. By harnessing these innovations, I aspire to create transformative tools and platforms that not only enhance our daily lives but also redefine how we interact with the digital world.
